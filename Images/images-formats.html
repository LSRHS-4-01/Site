GIF

GIFs are used for simple web-based graphics because of their low file size. They are restricted to 256 colors and should be used selectively.

JPG

JPGs are best used when working with photographs or graphics such as shadows or gradients that have complex effects. JPGs have a wide range of colors available, so you do not have to select a color palette.

PNG

There are two options when working with PNGs- PNG-8 and PNG-24. A PNG-8 is limited to the same types of files that you would use for GIFs. This can give you a smaller file size. A PNG-24 provides an image with much better quality. They can handle transparency much better than a GIF can.
